Web site critiques

Questions:

  1. Which site are you evaluating?
  2. What was your first impression of this site?
  3. How does this site organize the flow of information?
  4. Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you? Why?
  5. Does the site graphics/layout/multimedia enhance the site or detract from it? Why?
  6. How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?
  7. Was the color and size readable? If a background color or image was used, did it enhance or detract from the site?
  8. Did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was the navigation well done?
  9. Did the site have a color scheme or look and feel that was repeated on all the pages?
  10. How does the site respond to smaller window sizes (responsive design)?
  11. What does this site offer that was unusual or very well done?

Website Critique – Trader Joe’s

www.traderjoes.com
  1. Trader Joe’s - http://www.traderjoes.com/
  2. My first impression is that it looks clean and fun. I really liked the pictures of the food and noticed that right away.
  3. The top has links to sections and below it has new products and features. It’s really easy to follow and not feel lost in their website.
  4. It makes sense. Everything is where I thought it would be so I didn’t ever feel lost.
  5. The pictures and layout make the site better. It feels like they have their brand and it’s not super busy in their website.
  6. It loaded pretty fast, maybe only a couple seconds. I do think that is reasonable.
  7. Yes, the text was easy to read and colors matched their theme well and didn’t make it hard to see.
  8. Yes, there was a navigation bar at the top and a search box. It worked well for me.
  9. Yes, it uses red, white, and black across all pages so it did feel consistent.
  10. It works fine on smaller screens. The menu changes and the pictures resize.
  11. The store locator and “What’s New” section are really helpful. They make the site stand out for sure.
