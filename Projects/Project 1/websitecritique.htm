Web site critiques

This page contains my criques of Trader Joes

Question:

Which site are you evaluating?

Answer:

Trader Joes - http://www.traderjoes.com/

Question:

What was your first impression of this site?

Answer:

My first impression is that it looks clean and fun. I really liked the pictures of the food and noticed that right away

Question:

How does this site organize the flow of information?

Answer:

The top has links to sections and below it has new products and features. Its really easy to follow and not feel lost in their websiet

Question:

Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you? Why?

Answer:

It makes sense. Everything is where i thought it would be so I didnt ever feel lost.

Question:

does the site graphics/layout/mulitmedia enhance the site or detract from it? Why?

Answer:

The pictures and layout make the site better. It feels like thei have their brand and its not super busy in their website.

Question:

How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?

Answer:

It loaded pretty fast, maybe only a couple seconds. I do think that is reasonable.

Question:

Was the color and size readable? If a bacground color or image was used, did it enhance or detract from the site?

Answer:

Yes, the text was easy to read and colors matched their theme well and didnt make it hard to see.

Question:

did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was the naviagtion well done?

Answer:

Yes, there was a naviagtion bar at the top and a search box. it worked well for me.

did the site have a color scheme or look and feel that was repeated on all the pages?

Yes, it uses red, white, and black across all pages so it did feel consietent.

It works fine on smaller screens. The menu chnages and the pictures resize.

Question:

The store locator and "Whats New" section are really helpful. They make the site stand out for sure.
